The Profile

Antonio Ragusa — a life of many crafts

EntrepreneurProfessorAuthorCoachConsultantSpeakerComposer

Graduated cum laude in law and qualified as a lawyer, Antonio Ragusa holds an MBA from London’s Imperial College Business School, with further specialisation in marketing and communications at the London School of Economics.

He has founded several companies — among them the Rome Business School — and has taught managerial disciplines at universities and research centres including Roma Tre, Luiss Guido Carli and the University of Salamanca, after years of managerial experience within large international companies such as Enel and Trenitalia.

Author of numerous publications on organisational communication, marketing and human resources, he is the winner of international awards for his entrepreneurial work and his commitment to ethics-driven education on a global scale.

My Story

From Sicily to the world, one dream at a time.

“I’m often asked about my history and career, so I thought I’d share it with you here…”

I.

The Origins

Antonio was born in Sicily — the great island off the tip of southern Italy, a land rich in history, art, culture and natural beauty, yet rife with social difficulties. His parents instilled in him a sense of duty, the importance of knowledge, and the value of the equality of all human beings.

A lifelong love of culture, knowledge and music pushed him to travel, study abroad and explore other countries. By thirty, he had moved with his family to Rome — a choice made out of love for this globally unique city.

II.

An Idea

While teaching at universities and business schools, he sensed something missing: true care for students, know-how-oriented training, and openness to the international dimension. Convinced that the 2008 financial crisis was largely born of an unethical approach to business, he saw the need for education that placed ethics and social responsibility at its heart.

Thus the Rome Business School was born, upon a single motto — “Better Managers for a Better World.”

III.

The Entrepreneurial Journey

The school began with two students, little time and little money. In the early years Antonio did everything himself — teaching, marketing, administration — working until two in the morning, weekends included, for three years.

Little by little, the Rome Business School grew into one of the most international business schools in the world, welcoming students from over 140 countries.

IV.

Growth & Recognition

Schools opened in Africa; partnerships were established on every continent; investments followed in many countries. His work has been appreciated by Heads of State and Nobel laureates, and formally recognised on a planetary scale — appointment as Academician by AEREC, the New York Award 2018, and the Emilio Castelar Award 2019 in Seville.

V.

Believe in Your Dreams

How was all this possible? “A clear vision, passion, persistence, productivity, mental perspective, and the right people.” In life, it matters to do what one truly loves — and to work on oneself. Because there are no real limits, except those we set for ourselves.

“I hope my story can provide a stimulus to find the courage to believe in your dreams.”

Seven Disciplines

One life, many crafts.

The common denominator of Antonio Ragusa’s work is a love for knowledge and music — and the desire to share these passions with others, helping them express their talent and make their dreams come true.

No. I — Enterprise

Entrepreneur

An exciting challenge

Entrepreneurship is one of the most exciting, complex and stimulating challenges — a commitment that tests and develops every human quality: intuition, courage, determination, leadership. A path fraught with difficulty and risk, yet a source of great reward.

Continue reading

Antonio took up entrepreneurship after years as a professional and company manager — experience that taught him the mistakes to avoid, above all in human resources. To succeed, one must pick the best people and look after them properly.

His aspiration gave birth to the Rome Business School and, later, the Rome Business School Nigeria — established nationally and internationally within just a few years. Beyond education, he has invested in managerial consultancy, real estate, finance and services.

No. II — Letters

Author

The importance of writing

When thoughts are written down, they get a shot at immortality and a wider circulation. This is why Antonio has endeavoured to leave a trace of his ponderings and research through publication.

Continue reading

His main research fields are internal communication and internal marketing — the roles played by an organisation’s members in the activities that reverberate among clients and stakeholders: an essential, still underestimated aspect of management, approached in a multidisciplinary way with special attention to corporate culture and the cross-cultural perspective.

Lecturer

The pleasure of teaching

No. IV — The Stage

Speaker

The power of words

Words can literally change people’s lives. Antonio has spoken at — and organised — many international conferences and events: the Italy–Africa Business Forum and the Rome Innovation Summit in Rome, the AHRA Conference in Egypt, the Family Business Forum in Dubai.

Speaking topics
  • Entrepreneurship, family business & international economy
  • Marketing, corporate communication & business planning
  • Human resource management, education & training
  • Personal development & life planning — because success in any field is underpinned by our mental approaches and attitudes

No. V — Counsel

Consultant

Aiding the development of professionals and firms

Through his companies and in person, Antonio has engaged in managerial consultancy on an international scale — strictly orientated towards results, with a high degree of customisation tailored around each client’s specific needs.

Fields & method
  • Strategic & operational marketing, business planning, sales
  • Business development & corporate internationalisation
  • Personnel selection and hiring

Every engagement is designed around clearly identified performance metrics, rigorous implementation and excellent deliverables — drawing on operational best practices found at the global level.

No. VI — Growth

Coach

Unlocking full potential

“I love helping people to achieve their objectives. Often, we do not express our full potential.” Antonio’s coaching facilitates professional and personal development by making his concrete wealth of experience available — individually and in groups.

Continue reading

Coaching helps to focus on objectives, make important and difficult decisions, and realise plans more effectively — enabling the surer and swifter achievement of results in both professional and personal spheres. Areas of intervention: professional progression, personal development and life planning.

Time is one of our most precious resources. Contact Antonio to explore, with no commitment, the possibility of working together towards your growth.

No. VII — Music

Composer

An all-consuming passion

Guitar at twelve, then drums for many years, and a self-taught command of the piano. Antonio played with bands in Italy and abroad — and was selected, with the Apple Scruffs, to represent Italy at Liverpool’s Beatles Convention.

Continue reading

More than a performer, he is a composer: author of dozens of songs, registered with the S.I.A.E. — Italy’s copyright collecting agency — as both music composer and literary author. Among his ongoing projects, he is writing a musical.
